Grandma Doug's Good Old Fashioned Homemade
Peanut Butter & Jelly Samm'iches

What The Heck Is It?

Heaven wrapped between two slices of bread. A samm'ich conjured from beyond our reckoning. Mere mortal words are not powerful enough to describe this samm'ich. It would be foolish to even attempt to.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter from the Bubbling Bog of Stickiness
  • Extremeberry Jam stolen from the Radical Teens from the Cul-de-sac Of DOOM
  • Butter forged from the teat of the Ubercow
  • Bananas gifted from the treasury of her majesty, Queen Kong
  • Bread of some kind. From the grocery store, I guess. Sliced is good.

Directions

  1. Steel your nerves. You'll need 'em.
  2. Find your STRONGEST blade.
  3. Throw it away. It's not strong enough.
  4. Pack for your journey to the Peaks Of Sorrow.
    • Did you remember your extra underpants?
    • Because you always forget them.
    • Okay, but know we're not stopping to buy more.
  5. Find the Crying Horned Peak King.
  6. Cheer him up. He usually likes a good joke - just nothing dirty.
  7. For cheering him up successfully, he will give you his Bejewled Blade of +2 Spreading
  8. You are finally ready to begin. Give yourself a tiny high five.
  9. Return from the Peaks Of Sorrow, but take the scenic route.
  10. Take some nice pictures.
  11. Post those pictures to social media.
  12. Gather your ingredients and bind them to the table with leather straps
  13. Say the following incantation:
    • "ELQG WKHVH LQJUHGLHQWV! KROG WKHP WR WKH WDEOH! ELQG WKHVH LQJUHGLHQWV!!!"
  14. Take two slices of bread and lay them within the boundary of the alchemical circle.
  15. Have Alexa play your samm'ich crafting playlist.
  16. Grasp the Bejewled Blade of +2 Spreading
  17. Dip the Bejewled Blade of +2 Spreading in the Peanut Butter
  18. Ignore its screams of anguish.
  19. Force the Bejewled Blade of +2 Spreading to one of the slices of bread.
  20. Try not to get too much blood on the other slice of bread.
  21. Cover the slice of bread with whatever ammount of substance you were able to cleave from the body of the Peanut Butter
  22. Set camp and rest for the night. The hardest part is yet to come!
  23. As dawn breaks, put on your coolest shades and most fashionable outfit. The Extremeberry Jam can sense those too old and out of touch!
  24. Once you have calmed your fears and poppped your collar, open the Extremeberry Jam Jar.
  25. Oh no! You weren't cool enough!
  26. Try to fight the Extremeberry Jam with your Bejewled Blade of +2 Spreading
  27. That won't work, you dummy! You'll have to try something else!
  28. Try to tell the Extremeberry Jam all the Pokemon you know by name.
  29. It hates that! The Extremeberry Jam retreats back into its jar!
  30. Act quickly and scoop some out with your Bejewled Blade of +2 Spreading
  31. Smoosh a bunch on the other (hopefully) not bloody slice of bread.
  32. Close your eyes.
  33. Make sure they're closed
  34. Peel the Banana from the treasury of her majesty, Queen Kong
  35. Be careful not to peek, they're very bashful when naked.
  36. Slice the naked (now screaming) Banana on Peanut Butter coated slice of bread.
  37. Allow the Peanut Butter to hold the scrambling pieces to the bread slice
  38. Press the two slices together, ingredient laden sides together like they're kissing
    • Do NOT make kissey noises while you do this.
  39. Once connected, hold them in place with a vice.
  40. Prepare a fire.
  41. After your fire reaches the right color/smell, loose some Butter from the Ubercow into a cast silver skillet
  42. Do not succumb to the sirens call to touch the melted Butter.
  43. Release the samm'ich from the vice.
  44. Wrestle the samm'ich to the pan.
  45. Wait for the curses to release from the bread before flipping.
  46. After the samm'ich stops moving, remove from the pan. (This may take up to 7 days.)
  47. Enjoy Grandma Doug's Good Old Fashioned Homemade Peanut Butter & Jelly Samm'ich!
  48. Repeat process from step one, if you would like a second helping.
  49. You WILL want a second helping.
